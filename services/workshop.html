Our workshop

Our workshops seek to address the following issues by:

  • Building and strengthening through spiritual awareness and social awareness
  • Teaching principles and techniques to positively address emotional and physical circumstances
  • Providing experiences for mental, social, and academic growth

    • Beyond the workshops we seek to provide continuing emotional and social support to our young ladies throughout early adulthood. Workshop Goals:

  • Foster active and healthy lifestyle choices
  • Improve social and emotional health
  • Cultivate positive identity and character
  • Strengthen academic achievement
  • Provide awareness and guidance for college and career direction

    • We also plan to partner with Boys and Girls Club of Dallas County to enrich the lives of young ladies within Dallas County. We want to equip and strengthen at least 15 ladies in social/ emotional skills and to be more successful in school and to make better decisions in their day-to-day lives.

    Examples of the workshops are;

  • Discover Me: Ages 10-13
  • The Kings Daughter: Ages 14-17
  • You are No Ordinary Queen: Ages 18 -24
  • His Crown – Her Destiny: Ages 25 -30
  • Esther Extraordinaire: Ages 30 and up

    • 1) Discover Me (DM) Workshops Ages 10-13 Curriculum

  • Self-Esteem : The right kind of confidence can only be based on true self-worth- believing you are a valuable person to be respected and loved. The Bible has a great deal to say about how we are to think of ourselves and other personal value. We understand that without this deep sense of worth you can never be truly happy. Having a proper sense of your own personal identity leads to true inner confidence and stability. This is what we hope to instill in the young women we serve.
  • Social Grace : Youth will be coached on visual presentation, social etiquette, personal grooming, and table etiquette. The goal is to empower them to achieve their maximum potential by building confidence in their social skills.
  • Social Media isn’t So Social : The technology unit is designed to instill a sense of awareness and responsibility when handling technology. The goal is to empower our youth to behave morally, ethically, and socially, while using mobile devices and laptops, via the various popular internet social sites.
  • Health : The health unit will teach or focus on the importance of taking care of your body through exercise, good nutrition, and proper diet. We will also focus on the importance of distinguishing between healthy and unhealthy relationships.

    • 2) Princess – The Kings Daughter workshops Ages 14- 17 Topics/ Curriculum

  • My Faith and My Life : To learn the important role faith plays in directing and positively influencing our lives.
  • Authentically Me : To learn to value themselves as unique individuals and gain an understanding of the inner qualities they possess to contribute to God’s plan and impact their families, communities and world.
  • Beautiful Inside to the Core : ED participants learn the importance of placing a greater focus on the development of their inner person so that their outer person will properly reflect the characteristics and values they desire.
  • Become a Visionary : Learn to be proactive in making right decisions concerning their life. Students will be encouraged to think about their dreams, aspirations, goals enlighten their current gifts (natural), experience (knowledge, skills) and education (knowledge, skills) and then look at ways they may achieve them.
  • Recognize the Leader in Me : Learn accountability for ones actions and behavior assessing their current values and priorities; empower to make right, healthy decisions and identifying their strong positive character and personality traits: ways to develop them and adopting traits they don’t yet possess in order to excel in life and positively influence others.
  • Live Beyond Sexuality : Discuss dating and forming positive relationships with the opposite sex. ED participants will explore the role their sexuality plays in the everyday lives, and how forming a positive mindset regarding their sexuality can improve their lives.
  • Challenge My Thinking : Learning the connection between negative thinking patterns and negative behavior and learning to recognize and change them.
  • Mastering My Emotions : Learn to properly manage emotions and responses to the words and actions of others.
  • Money Matters : Develop a healthy mindset about money and its purpose. How to manage their money through proper planning and budgeting not becoming a slave to money and debt. Ways to multiply their money through giving and through various financial products.
