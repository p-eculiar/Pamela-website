Our project

Mentoring and Enrichment Workshops

Through our mentoring and enrichment workshops, we seek to enrich and transform the lives of youth and young adult girls who may be dealing with negative or stressful home environments, lack of emotional & educational support, negative peer pressure or transient living conditions, and/or homelessness. Girls who experience these conditions are often times at risk for educational setbacks, teen pregnancy, drug/alcohol abuse, and trouble with the law.

Obligation of the mentor

  • Willingness to commit to the mentoring relationship for the designated time period.
  • Willingness to meet by phone/videoconference with mentee once a month.
  • Willingness to correspond with mentee via email with a 48-hour response.
  • Willigness to share personal stories, experience and examples.
  • Willigness to help facilitate connections for mentee.
  • Willigness to mentee in identifying career development opportunities or project resources.
  • Willigness to provide feedback to the board about the program and areas for improvement.
  • Willigness to answer questions of future mentors.
